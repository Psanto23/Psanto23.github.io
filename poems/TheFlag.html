The Flag

There’s a flag in this town
And it won’t come down
No it won’t come down
No it won’t come down

They wave it around
To a raucous sound
From the people in town
No it won’t come down

Stake it into the ground
And walk into the crowd
Don’t let it fall down 
Make your flag waver proud

In this town
Where the flag don’t come down
And the blood don’t dry
And the stars don’t shine
Wave your flag high
With your hand to the sky
